RM

Kim Namjoon formerly stages named RM is a South Korean rapper, songwriter, composer, and producer. Kim Namjoon was born on the 12th of September, 1994 in Ilsan-gu, South Korea. As of 2021, he is 26 with 5 feet 11 inches tall in height. Kim is the main rapper of BTS and also is the leader. has released two mixtapes in 2015 and 2018 RM and Mono respectively as a solo artist.

V

Kim Taehyung V, also popularly known as V, is a popular BTS boy band member. He got interested in music and wanted to pursue professional singing when he was in elementary school. Later, he started learning the saxophone and passed an audition, and became a trainee at Big Hit Entertainment. V with other band members made their first debut in 2013 as BTS; No More Dream was the first track. He has also been credited for co-producing some songs as well along with J-Hope. V also writes songs, and a few notable mentions include Fun Boyz, Stigma. Later, he ventured into acting and was quite famous for his acting chops. V is also known to have coined a phrase used by UNICEF in collaboration with BTS for the anti-bullying campaign, “I purple you.” His popularity is widespread, so much so that in 2018, V was the most searched keyword.

Jungkook

Jungkook was born on September 1, 1997 (age 23 years; as in 2020) in Busan, South Korea. Jungkook’s real name is Jeon Jung-kook. He has studied Graduation (Art) from Seoul’s School of Performing Arts in 2017. There is not much information about their parents. He has a brother named Jeon Jung Hyun. Actually Euphoria is a song of a 9-minute short film that was released with an accompanying nine-minute short film “Love yourself” on April 5, 2018, as the introduction to the third part of BTS’.

Jin

Kim Seok-jin, also known as “Jin” is a singer-songwriter from South Korea and a vocalist of the noted seven-member South Korean K-pop boy band ‘BTS.’ Jin entered the music industry after being spotted by ‘Big Hit Entertainment’ and eventually made his debut with ‘BTS,’ in the band’s debut effort, ‘2 Cool 4 Skool.’ His first co-produced track, ‘Awake,’ was a solo single from second album of ‘BTS,’ ‘Wings.’ The song ‘Epiphany,’ which became part of the ‘BTS’ compilation album ‘Love Yourself: Answer’ and made its way to top spots on several international charts (including the fourth spot on the ‘Billboard World Digital Singles Chart’), was Jin’s second solo. He has garnered worldwide recognition as a member of ‘BTS.’ The band has reached new heights of success with time and is known for its global influence. Jin and the other members of ‘BTS’ have received several honors and awards as part of the group, including the fifth-class ‘Hwagwan Order of Cultural Merit’ awarded by South Korean president Moon Jae-in. Jin has collaborated on the song ‘It's Definitely You’ with fellow ‘BTS’ member V and has hosted shows such as ‘KBS Song Festival’ and ‘Music Bank.’

Suga

Min Yoon-gi (민윤기), better known by his stage names SUGA (슈가) and Agust D is a South Korean rapper-songwriter and record producer under Big Hit Music. He is a rapper of BTS, also he is notable for his large input in songwriting and is the major music producer between the members. As a solo artist, he has released two mixtapes: Agust D in 2016 and D-2 in 2020. In 2018, Suga was promoted to full member of the Korea Music Copyright Association. SUGA is responsible for writing, composing, arranging, mixing, and mastering his material. Over 100 registered songs are credited to him by the Korea Music Copyright Association. He is a pianist, and produces hiphop and R&B music.[13] His lyrics involve themes that are "full of dreams and hope," conceived with the intent of his music becoming "many people's strength."
